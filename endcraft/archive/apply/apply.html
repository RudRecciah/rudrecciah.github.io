APPLY

Welcome to the EndCraft staff application form! Here you can apply for staff if you feel as if you are a good fit for our team. Before applying, please make sure you meet the requirements below:

- You have a Discord account.
- You are 15 years of age or older.

If your application is denied, please wait 14 days before you re-apply. If you do not receive a response via email or Discord within 14 days, you were most likely denied. Anyone who re-applies before that period of time will be denied automatically. Contacting a staff member or the owner to review your application will lead to an instant denial of it. Be patient while your application is being reviewed. Please do not lie in your application; if you are caught, you will most likely be blacklisted from applying. Good luck!
